Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How does the 7-day free trial work?

When you sign up, you get full access to all courses and features for 7 days absolutely free. If you don't cancel within those 7 days, your subscription will automatically continue at ₹999/month. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends with no charges.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes! Upon successful completion of any course (including all assignments and passing the final assessment), you'll receive a verified certificate of completion that you can share on your LinkedIn profile, resume, or social media.

Are the courses self-paced or scheduled?

All courses are completely self-paced. You can learn at your own schedule, pause and resume whenever you want. Some courses also offer optional live sessions with instructors for Q&A, but these are always recorded for later viewing.

What if I need help during my learning?

We offer multiple support channels: 1) Course discussion forums where you can ask questions and get answers from instructors and fellow students, 2) 24/7 email support for technical issues, and 3) Weekly live Q&A sessions with course instructors.

Can I download course materials for offline viewing?

Yes, most course materials including videos, PDFs, and resources are available for download via our mobile app. This allows you to continue learning even when you're offline or on the go.
